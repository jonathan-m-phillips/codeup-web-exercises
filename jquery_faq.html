National Parks

Rocky Mountain National Park
1: One of the highest national parks in the nation.
2: The park's boundaries were established in 1915.
3: The Trail Ridge Road was built in the 1930's.
4: The peaks of the mountains may have snow while down in the plains will have summer temperatures.
5: The views are priceless.
6: The tallest peak in the park is 14,259 feet tall on Longs Peak.
7: There are elk, moose, mule deer, pronghorns, wolves, mountain sheep, mountain lions, and many more animals who live there.
8: The mountains and the wildlife bring peace.
9: The National Park is only one hour away from Denver, CO.
10: The Park is free to enter at night time.
